G.H. as Human Being Object: An Intervention in the Form of a Detective Novel

Norah Curran

Click here to view the full essay!

This paper, written by class member Norah Curran, predated our reading of The Passion According to G.H. It is a narratological intervention in the literary theory discussion of Lispector's work, using Tvetzan Todorov and Peter Brooks’ narratological theories to come to an understanding of G.H. as a protagonist in view of the book's existential themes.
